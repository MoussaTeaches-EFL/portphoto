About

"Photography is the art of freezing the world at its most luminous — the moment the ordinary becomes consecrated."

Based between the Swiss Alps and Coastal California, I've spent fifteen years chasing light across six continents. My work explores the quiet drama of natural landscapes — the tension between human scale and geological time.

I work with editorial clients, conservation organizations, and private collectors. Prints are produced on archival Hahnemühle paper, limited to 12 editions, signed and numbered.
